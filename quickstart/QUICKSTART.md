# Quickstart (15 minutes)

## Step 1 — Create your Exhibit Index
Open `exhibits/EXHIBIT_INDEX.md` and add 3–5 exhibits:
- court order(s)
- parenting schedule terms
- key communication thread(s)

## Step 2 — Start your Chronology
Open `casepack/02-chronology.md` and add:
- 5 most recent date-ordered events
- each with exhibit anchors

## Step 3 — Use templates
Add new entries using:
- `templates/TEMPLATE-event-entry.md`
- `templates/TEMPLATE-communication-entry.md`

## Step 4 — Weekly summary
Each week, fill:
- `templates/TEMPLATE-weekly-summary.md`
This becomes your clean “Water” layer.

## Step 5 — Remedies
Only after at least 2–3 weeks of Mirror entries, draft:
- `casepack/10-remedies-and-requests.md`
