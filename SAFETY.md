# Safety & De-escalation Standard

This template assumes the goal is stability and reduced conflict.

## Communication safety
- Use calm language
- Stick to schedules and logistics
- Avoid blame, insults, and speculation
- Use one topic per message when possible

## Documentation safety
- Write as if a judge will read it aloud
- Focus on observable facts
- Separate impact on the child from opinions about the other parent
- If you feel overwhelmed, pause and consult your attorney or a trusted support person
