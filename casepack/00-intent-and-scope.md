# Intent and Scope

## Intent
This casepack is maintained to:
- preserve accurate records
- reduce conflict and misunderstanding
- support stable parenting time and routines
- enable efficient review by counsel

## Scope
Included:
- parenting-time schedule and deviations
- exchange events
- communications (logistics/scheduling)
- school/activity logistics impacting routine
- travel-document logistics (if relevant)
- safety events (fact-only)

Excluded:
- insults/opinions
- speculation about motives
- private child identifiers
